Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2025
Effective Date: January 31, 2025

Marc Larouche ("we," "us," or "our") operates this interactive portfolio website (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site and interact with our features, including the AI-powered chatbot and job compatibility checker.

Key Points:
  • We collect minimal personal information
  • Your chatbot conversations are not stored or tracked
  • Job descriptions you paste are processed locally in your browser
  • We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
  • You have full control over your data

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you interact with our Site, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Contact Form Data: Name, email address, message content, and any other information you choose to include when contacting us
  • Job Description Text: Text you paste into the Job Fit Checker tool for compatibility analysis
  • Chatbot Interactions: Questions and messages you type into the chatbot interface

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns
  • Technical Data: IP address, referring URLs, access times
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: See our Cookie Policy below

1.3 What We DON'T Collect

Important: Our chatbot and job fit checker are designed with privacy in mind:
  • Chatbot conversations are NOT stored on any server
  • Job descriptions are processed locally in your browser
  • We do NOT track individual conversation content
  • We do NOT sell or share your personal information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 Site Functionality

  • Operate and maintain the interactive features of the Site
  • Process your chatbot queries and provide responses
  • Analyze job descriptions for compatibility matching
  • Improve user experience and Site performance

2.2 Communication

  • Respond to your inquiries and contact form submissions
  • Send you information you requested
  • Communicate about potential employment opportunities

2.3 Analytics and Improvement

  • Understand how visitors use the Site
  • Identify technical issues and improve performance
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns (no individual tracking)

3. How We Process Your Data

3.1 Chatbot Functionality

Our chatbot uses pattern-matching technology that operates entirely in your browser:

  • Local Processing: Your questions are matched against pre-programmed responses locally
  • No Storage: Conversation content is NOT saved to our servers
  • No Tracking: We do NOT monitor or record individual conversations
  • Session Only: Any conversation data exists only during your browser session

3.2 Job Fit Checker

The job compatibility analysis tool:

  • Browser-Based: All analysis happens in your browser using JavaScript
  • No Uploads: Job descriptions you paste are NOT uploaded to servers
  • Temporary Only: Text is cleared when you close or refresh the page
  • Keyword Matching: Uses local algorithms to compare skills and requirements

Data Type Retention Period

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation on how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time

6.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under CCPA:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of personal information collected, used, or shared
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we collected
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do NOT sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your rights

6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond to your request within:

  • 30 days for GDPR requests
  • 45 days for CCPA requests (with possible 45-day extension)
